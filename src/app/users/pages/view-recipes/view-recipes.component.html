Classic Margherita Pizza

Cuisine : Italian

Preperation Time

20 Minutes
Serving : 4

Cooking Time

10 Minutes
Mode of Cooking : Easy

Calorie per Serving

100

Ingredients Needed

  1. chicken
  2. oil
  3. salt
  4. water

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven Preheat your oven to 220°C (425°F). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven while preheating.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.Transfer the dough to a baking sheet or pizza pan (lightly oiled or dusted with cornmeal).
  3. Bake in the preheated oven for 12–15 minutes or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  4. Broil for Crispiness
